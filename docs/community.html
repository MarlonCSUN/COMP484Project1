The 86 Community

The SOCAL 86 Community is a group of enthusiasts who share a passion for the Toyota 86/ Subaru BRZ/ Scion FR-S sports car. The community is based in Southern California as the name suggests, and it hosts a variety of events and activities for memebers to connect with each other. The most popular events include car meets, track days, and group drives. The community also does have an Instagram page where it shares photos of the events, and updates on events. You can find the Instagram page here. All 86/BRZ/FR-S are welcome and it is quite unique to see the CTRL + C and CTRL + V the cars are yet they are all different in their own way.
