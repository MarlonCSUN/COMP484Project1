My Journey with the Eighty-Six

The history of the Toyota 86/Subaru BRZ/Scion FR-S is a long and storied one, with the car being developed as a joint effort between Toyota and Subaru. The car was first introduced in 2012 and has since become a beloved sports car among enthusiasts. I have been a fan of the chasis since 2016. My Aunt owned one which led to my brother owning one and that same car my brother owned becoming my first car. I have owned the car for several year and have made many modifications to it, which is documented in the modifications section of this website.

The Timeline

2018: The car officially becomes mine after my brother and I start thinking about what I want to do with the car
2019: I've already started making modifications to the car and have made a list of modifications I want to do to the car.
2020: COVID-19 hits and I am not able to work on the car as much as I would like to. It was a very slow year for the car.
2021: I start working on the car again and make some progress on the suspension and engine modifications.
2022: I snag a crazy deal on a JDL Turbo Kit and decide to go for it. I now start preparing the car for the kit.
2023: After a long wait, the turbo kit is officially installed with all necessary supporting modifications.
2024: Unfortunately, the car suffers a catastrophic engine failure and I have to rebuild the engine.
2025: The engine is rebuilt and the tuning process is finished, the car is back on the road and running better than ever.
However I am forced to get another car for daily use due to the extensive list of modifications on the car.
