Welcome to my personal car blog!

I share my passion for cars and automotive knowledge through this blog.
I am particularly sharing my journey with my 2013 Toyota 86, which I have owned for several years and have made many modifications to. I am also sharing some of the general history of the car as well as the my personal history with the car in the History Section of this website. I am also sharing some information regarding the Southern California 86 community in the Community Section of this website. In my last section I am sharing the modifications I have made to the car as well as some of my future plans for the car. This website is a project for my COMP 484 class and is meant to be a fun way for me to share my passion for cars.

About Me
